About Us

Special Film Company is a Finland based production company founded by young, fresh and ambitious freelance movie makers who decided it was time to build something of our own. Independent movie production is the name of our game, our plans are solid and our goal is simple: make better movies than anybody else. Our hallmarks are visual flair and ballsy, uncompromising stories – we are always willing to tackle Special, risky subjects to deliver something you really haven’t seen before.
